# Digital Insurgency — 30-Day Substack Series (Plan)

Goal: one post/day for 30 days, each delivering a **nugget** from the book + an
**equation in LaTeX** + an **SPZ-closer** riff, with **buy links at the top**, to
drive book sales. Drafts built first; you review/schedule.
