# At a glance

The Last Human CEO — a full-cast audiobook by Space Pirate Zero (Greg Chambers). An original Spaceship Alpha 9 production, written, scored, and voiced in-house.

  • FORMAT   Full-cast audiobook / serialized audio drama
  • LENGTH   29 episodes · ~12.6 hours
  • GENRE   Literary fiction · satire · tragic dark comedy · techno-thriller
  • RATING   Explicit
  • COMPS   Succession × Flowers for Algernon × Twilight Zone

In 2027, as corporate boards swap their CEOs for cheaper, scandal-proof AI, the last human chief executive in the Fortune 100 wages a manic, coke-fueled crusade to prove a person still belongs in the chair — until the machine taking his job quietly uncovers the thing he buried that got two people killed.
