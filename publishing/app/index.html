# The Book

Succession meets Flowers for Algernon, by way of a Twilight Zone broadcast.

Prescott “Cope” Mercer IV runs a 95-year-old Atlanta mayonnaise-to-insurance empire his great-grandfather founded. He is the last human CEO in the consumer economy — every rival has handed the chair to an AI that costs a tenth of a human, never lies, never has an affair, and never needs a scandal managed. When an activist investor moves to replace him with a machine, Cope spirals into a grandiose, cocaine-fueled campaign to prove his “Human Premium.” The tragedy: the machine reads the room better than he does; it just doesn’t care what it reads.

Narrated by Space Pirate Zero, transmitting the whole tale from the salvage decks — a full-cast audio drama about automation, accountability, and the last man to confuse a throne for a soul.

Contains strong language, drug use, sexual content, and depictions of suicide.
