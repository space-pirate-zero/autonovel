## SITREP
- Situation: the shadow system has run read-only for 3 months — 2.4M transactions
  observed, 847 anomalies the legacy missed, Sarah's team 63% faster.
- Complication: shadow mode is safe but invisible; the enterprise can turn you off
  and nothing changes. To become irreplaceable you must go live — and expose
  yourself to the immune system.
- Main Point: the transition from Shadow to System of Record must be triggered by
  a crisis that makes the status quo more dangerous than the change. You don't
  create the crisis. You wait. And when it comes, you're the only one ready.

exposure held to $127K. BISHOP arrives 30 seconds later, badge perfectly STRAIGHT
for the only time in the book. The CIO, 40 minutes later: "How fast can you start?"
The risk has inverted. She's no longer a vendor. She's infrastructure. The oxygen.
