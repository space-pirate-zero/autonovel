# Standard: SPZ Brand (applies to every asset)

One brand across every book, cover, site, and social asset: **SPZ / Space Pirate
Zero**. The canonical kit is `brand/spz/` — **do not fork it or invent variants.**
To change the brand, edit `brand/spz/build_brand_kit.py` and redeploy the one
`brand_kit.html` artifact. This doc is the quick-reference the generators encode;
`brand/spz/*.json` is the source of truth.

## Color (from `brand/spz/palette.json`)

Mood: *industrial-goth cyberpunk noir — near-black void, hot neon, cold signal,
newsprint grit.*

| Token | Hex | RGB | Role |
|---|---|---|---|
| `void` | `#030303` | 3, 3, 3 | Primary background. Near-black, never pure black. Everything sits on it. |
| `pink` | `#FF1493` | 255, 20, 147 | Primary accent — hot magenta. Titles, CTAs, the title glow, the SPZ logotype. |
| `cyan` | `#00F0FF` | 0, 240, 255 | Cold signal. Kickers, secondary accent, waveform's cool end. |
| `paper` | `#E8E8E8` | 232, 232, 232 | Primary text on void. |
| `muted` | `#8A90A0` | 138, 144, 160 | Secondary/label text, sub-lines. |

Waveforms and gradient blooms go **cyan → pink**. Neon glow = the accent color
blurred behind the text at a pulsed alpha.

## Type (fonts in `fonts/`)

| Family | Use |
|---|---|
| **Orbitron** (500/700/900) | Display — titles, "NOW STREAMING", big lockups. |
| **Space Grotesk** (300/400/500/700) | Body / UI / hooks / sub-lines. |
| **JetBrains Mono** (400/700) | Kickers, the domain, "A SPACE PIRATE ZERO TRANSMISSION", code/terminal texture. |

Generators load these from the repo-root `fonts/` dir. Because ffmpeg here has no
`drawtext`, **all** on-asset text is drawn with Pillow using these TTFs.

## Voice & tagline (from `brand/spz/brand.json`)

- **Tagline:** *Signal finds signal.*
- **Essence:** *Diogenes with a laptop — deface the counterfeit, live honest.*
  Snarky, punk, Atlanta-trap, hacker. Cyberpunk-noir in look, industrial-goth in
  sound, bar-at-midnight in voice.
- **Copy voice:** cinematic, a little heartbroken; punches down at counterfeit
  power, never at people. Every release is *"a Space Pirate Zero transmission."*

## Sonic (from `brand/spz/sonic.json`)

Theme music (ElevenLabs Music) and audiobook scoring must match the industrial-goth
sonic identity. The show theme and the ducked title-card intro are generated by
each book's `gen_theme.py` against this spec.

## The rule

Any new generator, cover, page, or asset **consumes these tokens** — it does not
define its own. If you find yourself hard-coding a color that isn't above or
reaching for a font that isn't in `fonts/`, stop: you're drifting off-brand.
