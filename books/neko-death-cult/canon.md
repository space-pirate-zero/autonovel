# Canon — The Maneki Neko Death Cult *(working title)*

Hard facts and evaluator constraints. If a draft contradicts anything here, the
draft is wrong.

## Hard facts (NEVER contradict)
- SPZ is the **protagonist and first-person narrator**, not the author persona.
- The Advent Calendar of Death has **24 doors**, found at a dollar store in
  little Serbia. Shumate found it with him.
- The calendar **follows him across every jump** — his one constant object and
  crude console. It was on the nightstand at the Europa (Ep 1→2). Opened doors stay
  opened/emptied; he works them roughly in order, one per episode.
- Each door = one death cocktail (a real artist's fatal formula) = one jump.
- One door is **sealed** (Hemingway — a shotgun, not a cocktail): the bet you
  can't come back from.
- Door 1 (the Belushi door) = the source blog: the Vault (Atlanta, 2003) → the
  motel (Cult contact) → the Europa Hotel, Belfast (first clean landing).
- The Europa Hotel is "the world's most bombed hotel," in Belfast.
- The Maneki Neko Death Cult **whispers; it never kills.** Every death is self-inflicted
  by the mark.
- The Cult's two agents want **nothing** from SPZ — he is a monitored data point.
- The mantra, verbatim: **"Don't let the bastards grind you down to beige."**
- Cosmic Swing Kat **dances** to jump; SPZ **dies** to jump.
- SPZ and Kat meet **out of order.** She is dissolving into pure signal.

## The physics (see physics.md — the speculative core)
- The slipstream runs on **Reality Transurfing** physics: infinite **already-filmed
  tracks** (the Filmstrip); you **slide between** them by inner state, you never
  create them.
- It's **track travel, not time travel.** Every era coexists in the block, so he can
  land in any *when*. "Forward only / never rewind" binds his **own** thread (no
  re-entering a lived groove, no un-living), NOT the calendar — which is how he
  reaches "past" eras and meets Kat out of order. (Two clocks; see physics.md.)
- **Two ways to slide:** the **Clean Slide** (Kat — zero importance / neutral state
  / least resistance) and the **Brute Slide** (SPZ — max excess potential via
  death; the calendar is an excess-potential detonator). His arc = learning hers.
- **The Balancing** does all the killing; the Cult are **pendulums** that only
  inflate importance. **Arrive how you died** = the death's signature sets the
  landing groove. **Kat dissolves** = decoherence into the carrier wave (pure
  signal). The five Laws of the Slipstream in physics.md are evaluator constraints.

## The slipstream — laws (from world.md, restated as constraints)
1. Death is the only door (one measured OD = one jump).
2. **Never rewind the tape.** His *personal* clock is forward-only: no re-entering
   a lived groove, no un-living, no do-overs, ever. The *world's* clock is open —
   all eras coexist, so he can land in any *when* (track travel, not time travel).
   The single most important rule — breaking it breaks the book.
3. Arrive how you died — the cocktail colors era/city/vibe; saint and destination
   must agree per door.
4. Signal finds signal — slippers tuned alike keep colliding.
5. The fog lifts with mastery — clean landings are earned (the Door-1 Europa
   landing was a one-time gift/bait).
