# *Signal Finds Signal* — Credits

**A companion album to *The Maneki Neko Death Cult*.**

## Performance (in-world)

- **SPZ (Space Pirate Zero)** — lead vocal; the crooner. Every song is sung *to* Kat.
- **Cosmic Swing Kat** — answering vocal; the call-and-response. Kat is **Daniela**.

## Writing & concept

- Songs, lyrics, and album concept — the SPZ / Space Pirate Zero universe.
- Each door's era, conceit, and spoken samples derive from that door's episode; the canonical per-track brief is in [`../../album.md`](../../album.md).

## Production

- **Vocal generation:** Suno (Advanced mode) — one style + lyric sheet per track, from [`../../suno_parts/`](../../suno_parts/).
- **Sampling & master:** `tools/produce_song.py` — demucs vocal separation to find vocal-free pockets, spoken samples placed intro / mid / haunted-outro, sidechain ducking, mastered to ~-10 LUFS.
- **Spoken samples:** the show's own SPZ & Kat lines, from [`../samples/`](../samples/).

## The dedication

For **Daniela**. The record is hers — she is the signal the signal finds.
