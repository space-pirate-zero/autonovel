- **Mood:** lofi industrial goth meets a torch-song tenderness — neon-noir, rain-slick, a warm human glow inside cold machinery.
- **Motif options:** a single lit doorway in the dark (the 24 doors); two signals / waveforms finding each other; a maneki-neko silhouette dissolving into static; a cabaret light bleeding through a server-room grille.
- **Palette:** SPZ brand — void black `#030303`, hot pink `#FF1493`, cyan `#00F0FF`, paper `#E8E8E8`. (See `brand/spz/palette.json`.)
- **Type:** Orbitron / Space Grotesk (SPZ brand typography). Album title prominent; subtitle: *the companion album to The Maneki Neko Death Cult*.
- **Feel:** it should read as a love letter disguised as a death-cult record.
