**They wheel the future in, and it smiles cruelly.**

Static, then that little pop that means I found you again. Here's our dark little fable of the last human boss, told from the salvage decks by yours truly. So far our man's traded warnings for applause, called his last hope a slogan — "The Human Premium" — and stumbled toward a camera his own people begged him to skip. Now they wheel the future into the boardroom, and it does the cruelest thing imaginable: it writes a layoff letter kinder than any man ever could. Stay close to the set.
