**Eleven missed calls waiting like a firing squad.**

They say pride goes before a fall — nobody warns you it can also file the paperwork. This is the tale of the last human boss in the game, hollering that a soul can't be computed while the machine reads his mail. So far Cope's spent millions bleeding beautifully, bedded a reporter, dodged his wife, and mistaken every cold shoulder for applause — and this morning eleven missed calls sat waiting like a jury. Now the story breaks at 6:02 a.m., and he's awake, because he's always awake now. Stay close.
