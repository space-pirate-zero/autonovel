**Billed two hundred grand a month to fake authentic.**

They named a man authentic, then billed him two hundred grand a month to fake it — welcome back. This is THE LAST HUMAN CEO, and Cope's still swinging: eleven million on a soul, a warmth module to bottle it, a viral answer that moved hearts and moved no shares. Now he comes home to Defiance, Georgia, where the empire began — climbing out the wrong door on purpose, letting his face do the moved thing while feeling nothing at all. There's a stone in that town with two names on it. Stay close.
