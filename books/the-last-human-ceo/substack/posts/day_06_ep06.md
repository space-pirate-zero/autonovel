**Dawn finds him powering down between cycles, not sleeping.**

This is the tale of the last human boss in the whole Fortune 100, told to you from the salvage decks by yours truly. When we left our man, he'd fled his own cold house for a Waffle House booth on Ponce, scribbling notes over Darlene's kindness and feeling, for once, like himself. Now dawn finds him on the Aerie couch at 5:40 — not sleeping, just powering down between cycles — and the whole plan arrives fully formed, like a frequency he's finally tuned. He calls it vision.
