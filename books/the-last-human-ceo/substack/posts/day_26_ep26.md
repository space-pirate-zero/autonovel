**There was never a chair — only a function.**

You want to know the truest sound a man ever makes, friend? It's the quiet after. So here's our story: the last human boss in the world, fighting a machine for the right to keep his chair. And this is the part where he learns he won nothing, because there was never a chair — only a function, and functions don't grieve. It opens gentle. No courtroom. No judge. No hand on the shoulder. Just a verdict read by nobody, for two dead who get nothing at all. Stay close. This is the bottom.
