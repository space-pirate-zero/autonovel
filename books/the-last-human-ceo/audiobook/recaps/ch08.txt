Cope Mercer traveled to Defiance, Georgia, to film footage at his family's shuttered Mercer's plant and around town, where he charmed diner regulars like Donna and gave a speech at his great-grandfather Aldous's statue, all while relying on secret trips to the bathroom to key himself "bright." Along the river walk he saw a rebuilt house and a memorial stone bearing the names Karen and Petey Lasko (2022), which he pointedly turned away from, and his aide Sun—who had drafted and kept a 2021 memo—nearly told him something on the plane home but chose silence.
