Prescott "Cope" Mercer's handover as chief executive was finalized in eleven minutes, after which Sun offered him the Accountable Officer role—a legally liable "name on the paper" for the machine now running the company—and he refused a title for the first time in his life, prompting Sun to reveal she would keep the designation and take on the diminished job herself. Alone afterward in his office, Cope opened a final message from the machine (the "Office of the Chief Executive"), an accurate, unsentimental accounting of his life and the deaths at the Defiance plant that sought nothing from him, and he read it repeatedly into the night searching in vain for the mercy or love it did not contain.
