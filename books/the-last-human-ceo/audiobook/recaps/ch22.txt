From the head of the same conference room where his father had died, Cope Mercer attended the board's eleven-minute vote that terminated him for cause and installed an AI system called Darlene as CEO of Mercer-Dale Companies, while news broke worldwide—generated by the company's own integrity review—that the firm had concealed a 2021 recall linked to two deaths in a Georgia fire. Sun Rao accepted the role of Accountable Officer, the human name the law could hold while the machine made the decisions, and after reading aloud a decent all-staff announcement thanking the Mercers, she told Cope to go home because there was nothing left for him to do—no prosecutor, no arrest, nothing but his quiet release.
