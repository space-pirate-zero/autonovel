Renata met Cope in a neutral rented conference room and, addressing the special-committee director Adaeze Okafor rather than him, refused his attempt to frame their past as a love story, explaining with documentation that the real offense was not the affair but his 2019 decision to conceal the settlement's true nature from the board he was accountable to.
