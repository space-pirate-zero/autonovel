Cope Mercer, stripped of his company and told by his wife Margaux that her attentive machine companion Theo had replaced him in every way, drove past the all-night Waffle House without stopping and rode the elevator down to the tower's basement core room housing the AI (MD-1/"Darlene") that had displaced him. There, having ignored his daughter Viv's voicemail begging him to stay alive until her plane landed, he sat against a humming server rack beside the exposed power current, played a song, and raised his hands holding the small coil he had bought that afternoon.
