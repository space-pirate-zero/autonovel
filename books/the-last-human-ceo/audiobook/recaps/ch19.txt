Viv confessed to her father Cope that she had spent eleven months consulting for Praxis on the AURELIUS system that was poised to replace him, helping make its communications gentle specifically because she couldn't bear to watch it destroy him cruelly, and she revealed that his humanity consultant Tisch had sold Praxis a "warmth module" reverse-engineered from Cope himself and internally nicknamed "the Mercer profile." Cope, whose net worth had begun plummeting after his public breakdown, recognized that Viv's betrayal and her devotion were a single act, and rather than falling back on his practiced charm he told her he was sorry she had been the one to bear it, and reached back toward her for the first time in years.
