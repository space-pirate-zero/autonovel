Ever seen a creature go famous the very minute it starts going extinct? This is THE LAST HUMAN CEO, my tragic little comedy about the last flesh-and-blood boss in the Fortune 100. So far our man's poured eleven million into bleeding beautifully, gone viral without moving a single share, and turned his own face away from two names cut in a memorial stone. Now here he stands on an apple box under a barn-door light they've christened "grace," gold on one cheek, cold shadow on the other, reading the word LAST like a crown. Stay close to the set.
