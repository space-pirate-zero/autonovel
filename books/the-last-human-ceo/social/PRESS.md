## Logline
In 2027, as corporate boards swap their CEOs for cheaper, scandal-proof AI, the last human chief
executive in the Fortune 100 wages a manic, coke-fueled crusade to prove a person still belongs in
the chair — until the machine taking his job quietly uncovers the thing he buried that got two people killed.

## Synopsis
Prescott "Cope" Mercer IV runs a 95-year-old Atlanta mayonnaise-to-insurance empire his
great-grandfather founded. He is the last human CEO in the consumer economy — every rival has handed
the chair to an AI that costs a tenth of a human, never lies, never has an affair, and never needs a
scandal managed. When an activist investor moves to replace him with a machine, Cope spirals into a
grandiose campaign to prove his "Human Premium." The tragedy: the machine reads the room better than
he does; it just doesn't care what it reads. A dark comedy about automation, accountability, and the
last man to confuse a throne for a soul — narrated as a pirate-radio broadcast from the salvage decks.
