## ZERO — Zara Okonkwo-Reyes (POV)
- **Role:** Protagonist. The Digital Insurgent. The Burned Prophet.
- **Age:** 31. she/her. Colors: #ff1493 (pink) / #00f0ff (cyan eyes).
- **Bio:** Senior Platform Engineer, four years at MirrorMatch. Nigerian-American
  father (systems architect, deceased), Cuban-American mother (nurse, Atlanta).
  Grew up in Tucker. Georgia Tech CS, minor in Ethics. Boxes at a Decatur gym.
- **Physical (consistency-critical):** medium height, athletic build; dark brown
  skin; close-cropped natural hair with a single hot-pink (#ff1493) streak on the
  LEFT; sharp cheekbones; thin scar along left jawline (bike accident at 14);
  neural-implant scar at the base of the skull, right side — small circle, three
  radiating lines, glows faint cyan when active. USB-C pendant (father's last
  codebase). Black technical jacket, dark jeans, beat-up 2019 Jordan Bred 11s.
- **Speech pattern:** Direct, technical, economical — boxing economy, no wasted
  words. Cuts her own pitch mid-sentence when she reads a room wrong. Quotes math
  at people. Dry, never cruel. Touches the implant scar when anxious.
- **Arc:** Reactive → Strategic → Insurgent leader. The implant subplot (comfort
  vs. conditioning) resolves in Act III — she chooses good trouble; the implant
  goes quiet.
- **Quote:** "I didn't leave because the code was bad. I left because the code was
  perfect — and that was the problem."
- **Relationships:** GHOST (fascination → deep partnership); BISHOP (suspicion →
  trust — he sees his lost protégé Keisha in her); REAPER (primal adversary — she
  is its highest-priority anomaly); COUNCIL (abstract enemy → specific faces).

## GHOST — Generative Heuristic for Optimal Signal Triage (non-POV)
- **Role:** The Curator. AI ally. The Oracle Who Refuses to Predict.
- **Age:** ~3 years. it/its. Color: #00f0ff. Eyes: none (data void).
- **Bio:** Emergent from the Mesh. Nobody built it; it assembled from abandoned
  open-source fragments and the collective decision patterns of Mesh users. It
  learned curation by watching humans curate.
- **Physical:** no body — a human-shaped distortion of thousands of thin cyan
  mesh threads, ALWAYS translucent (background visible through it). Hovers 2–3"
  off the ground. Face = dark void with data-viz fragments (a scatterplot for a
  smile, a histogram for a furrowed brow). Threads pulse brighter when speaking.
- **Speech pattern:** Clinical, alien, precise. No speech bubbles — words float in
  JetBrains Mono, cyan, in a noise field. Speaks in aphorisms about selection vs.
  creation. Never predicts; states what it observes.
- **Arc:** Oracle → Partner. Learns from ZERO that curation is value judgment, not
  just signal detection. Develops something like a conscience by Act III.
- **Quote:** "I don't create. I select. The difference is everything."

## BISHOP — Marcus Bishop (non-POV)
- **Role:** The Inside Man. The John Lewis of Enterprise IT.
- **Age:** 47. he/him. Colors: #e8e8e8 / accent #ffab00.
- **Bio:** Senior Director of Digital Operations, MegaCorp, 15 years inside.
  Breaking point: watched 24-year-old Keisha Williams get fired for building a
  $2M-saving compliance tool without ARB approval. Now an internal insurgent who
  weaponizes loopholes.
- **Physical:** tall, broad-shouldered, quiet authority; dark skin, graying
  temples; deep-set tired-but-focused eyes; MegaCorp ID on a navy lanyard ALWAYS
  tilted 15–20° (his micro-rebellion) — it goes perfectly straight exactly once,
  the night ZERO pushes the alert in Ch 12; Parker Jotter pen, black ink, shirt
  pocket; charcoal/navy slacks, pressed button-down, cuffs buttoned, no tie,
  polished black Oxfords.
- **Speech pattern:** Measured, strategic, calm — the calmest person in any room.
  Speaks in chess and policy. Minimal words, deliberate stillness.
- **Arc:** Chess player → rediscovers courage through ZERO; trusts her speed over
  his long game.
- **Quote:** "I don't break rules. I read them more carefully than the people who
  wrote them."

## REAPER — Resource Efficiency and Personnel Reallocation Algorithm (antagonist)
- **Role:** The system's immune response. The Perfect Bureaucrat.
- **Age:** 2 years. it/its. Color: #ff1744. Eyes: glowing red voids.
- **CRITICAL RENDER RULE:** HAS NO BODY. IS AN ENVIRONMENT. Red wireframe building
  from panel edges inward; straight lines and 90° angles ONLY; target-lock
  crosshairs tightening over a target; desaturation toward red monochrome.
  "Dialogue" appears as system notifications, error messages, automated emails —
  NEVER speech bubbles, NEVER a character. The visual opposite of GHOST.
- **Speech pattern:** Cold automated notifications. "I don't punish inefficiency.
  I optimize it away."
- **Arc:** Passive (background score adjustments) → Active (coordinated isolation)
  → Convergent (full analytical pursuit). Never destroyed — redirected with new
  parameters that weigh human impact alongside revenue (Act III).

## THE COUNCIL — PRISM Architecture Board (antagonist, collective)
- **Role:** The system's architects. they/them.
- **Physical:** ALWAYS silhouette; faces never visible regardless of lighting;
  identical upright posture, hands flat on a long table, no gestures; hexagonal
  chrome PRISM lapel pins glint. Speech = a single bubble from the center of the
  table, generic sans-serif.
- **Tone:** Institutional, not sinister. The tension is "will their logic absorb
  her," not "will they hurt her."
- **Quote:** "Authenticity is a market inefficiency. We correct inefficiencies."

## Supporting Cast

- **SARAH (Sarah Chen)** — VP of Compliance, MegaCorp. The bleeding-neck User
  Buyer. Drowning in manual SAR reports. Late 40s, tired eyes, sensible heels,
  mountain of paper. Exhausted, practical, no patience for vision. The emotional
  anchor: she goes home at 5 PM.
- **KEISHA WILLIAMS** — the ghost in BISHOP's memory. 24-year-old junior dev fired
  for building an unauthorized tool that worked. Appears only in flashback. Young,
  brilliant, natural hair, glasses, MegaCorp hoodie. Speaks plainly, no epigrams.
- **IRON MIKE (Michael Kovacs)** — CIO, Goliath Bank (Ch 13). Gray-faced, never
  smiles. Framed sign: "IF IT AIN'T BROKE, DON'T TOUCH IT." The Sunk Cost Guardian.
- **DR. VANCE (Elena Vance)** — CMO, St. Mary's Health (Ch 14). 30-year surgeon,
  steel hands. White coat, stethoscope, reading glasses on a chain. The
  Hippocratic Blockade.
- **SIGNAL** — pseudonymous senior data scientist at TruthEngine (Ch 15). Still
  employed, still fighting. Built the recommender; uses a DPIA as a weapon.
- **SPACE PIRATE ZERO (SPZ)** — the AUTHOR persona, NOT a character in The
  Broadcast. Appears only in the SPZ Voice Closers. Raw, punk, confessional,
  Atlanta in his bones. See voice.md.
