# Digital Insurgency — Episode Map (14-Episode Field Course)

*The 2nd-Edition re-cut. Prologue + 16 chapters + 2 appendices → 14 episodes
across 3 modules. Each episode is a complete lesson; the sequence is the arc.*

Runtime targets are for the recorded read (deferred). Word counts are the 1E
source; 2E scripts adapt them for the ear.

---

## MODULE 0 — ORIENTATION

### Ep 01 — Orientation: The Mirror Test
- **Source:** Prologue ("The Portrait in the Server Room") + Five Claims framing
- **Boss:** *You, before you start.*
- **Physics:** none yet — this is the frame. Introduces the Dorian Gray portrait
  and the two pillars (The Physics / The Broadcast).
- **Beat:** 3:14 AM, MirrorMatch server room. ZERO finds the second dashboard —
  `MATCH_DELAY_ENGINE`, `LONELINESS_EXPLOITATION_INDEX` — sees her own handle in
  the git blame, lays her badge on the keyboard, walks into the Atlanta night.
- **The lesson (Mirror Test):** Before you smuggle anything into a company, look
  at what you're smuggling. A cooler UI on an exploitation engine makes you a
  decorator, not an insurgent. Look at the engine, not the gradient.
- **Course frame:** States the **Five Claims**. Hands over the through-line: the
  portrait can heal, four percent at a time.
- **Field assignment:** Find your own second dashboard. Name the engine under the
  gradient you ship.
- **Runtime:** ~18 min · **Status:** ✅ script locked (reference template)

## MODULE I — THE PHYSICS *(Act 1: escape & discovery)*

### Ep 02 — The Rewrite
- **Source:** Ch 1 ("The Rewrite" / *Every Line of Code Has an Expiration Date*)
- **Boss:** The "We'll Get to It" VP
- **Physics:** `R_ext` Extinction Rate · `A_hl` Authenticity Half-Life
- **Beat:** The Graveyard of dead SaaS on Peachtree. GHOST flickers in for the
  first time: "They didn't die because they were bad. They died because they were
  generic. Only the weird survives." Plant: the implant glows.
- **Claim:** #1 (the rewrite is coming) + #2 (authenticity is the currency)
- **Field assignment:** Compute your `A_hl`. Is your work replicable by Tuesday?
- **Runtime:** ~22 min · **Status:** ◻ drafting

### Ep 03 — The Physics of Stagnation
- **Source:** Ch 2 ("The Physics of Stagnation" / *The Gravity Well*)
- **Boss:** The Sunk-Cost Guardian (CIO)
- **Physics:** `F_g` Vendor Gravity · `E_tax` Entropy Tax · `μ_leg` Legacy
  Friction · `D_comp` Tech-Debt Compound Rate · `F_cp` Consensus Paralysis
- **Beat:** ZERO tries the front door — 47 forms, PRISM auto-rejects her. BISHOP
  sees the rejection queue, recognizes the MirrorMatch resignation, slips her a
  back-channel frequency. First contact. Vitamin / Painkiller / Tourniquet.
- **Claim:** #3 (the enterprise is physics)
- **Field assignment:** Score one stuck initiative on the Vitamin/Painkiller/
  Tourniquet hierarchy. Sell only tourniquets.
- **Runtime:** ~26 min · **Status:** ◻ drafting

### Ep 04 — The Curator's Edge & the Psychology of the Blockade
- **Source:** Ch 3 ("The Curator's Edge") + Ch 4 ("The Psychology of the Blockade")
- **Boss:** The Algorithm Evangelist → the Faction Boss (Technocrat/Bureaucrat/Warlord)
- **Physics:** `V_curator` Curator Value · `T_authentic` Authenticity Token ·
  `RT` Risk Tax · `DF` Decision Fatigue
- **Beat:** GHOST shows ZERO the Feed — infinite soulless AI content — and the
  0.01% with a human fingerprint. Then Conf Room 14-B: she watches CIO Kessler's
  amygdala fire at "transform," pivots to "refinance," his shoulders drop. The
  physics of fear. BISHOP watches through glass.
- **Claim:** #2 + #4 (the immune system reacts to threat words)
- **Field assignment:** Rewrite your pitch to lower the listener's `RT`. Swap one
  fear-word for a safe-word.
- **Runtime:** ~28 min · **Status:** ◻ drafting

### Ep 05 — The Portrait of Dorian App *(ethics keystone)*
- **Source:** Ch 5 ("The Portrait of Dorian App" / *The Soul-Cost of Building
  the Wrong Thing*)
- **Boss:** The Growth Hacker
- **Physics:** `D_gray` Dorian Gray Index · `S_soul` Soul-Cost. The Ethical Sidecar.
- **Beat:** Flashback, six months before the Prologue. MirrorMatch war room. A/B
  test: honest notification vs. manufactured scarcity. Scarcity wins, +23%. ZERO
  watches the user-research videos — real suffering — sees the portrait in the
  mirror. The day she decided to quit.
- **Claim:** the Dorian Gray metaphor made literal — the moral core of the course.
- **Field assignment:** Run the Mirror Test on your current sprint. Compute a rough
  `D_gray`. If it's climbing, build the Ethical Sidecar.
- **Runtime:** ~26 min · **Status:** ◻ drafting

## MODULE II — THE PROTOCOL *(Act 2: infiltration & growth)*

### Ep 06 — Architecture as Camouflage
- **Source:** Ch 6 ("Architecture as Camouflage" / *Inception Engineering*)
- **Boss:** The Enterprise Architect
- **Physics:** `R_r` Reversibility Ratio (the kill switch). The Strangler Fig.
- **Beat:** Diner on Ponce, 11 PM. BISHOP relabels ZERO's architecture in boring
  ARB vocabulary — "AI Inference Layer" → "Data Validation Service." "Make them
  think they designed it."
- **Claim:** #4 (bypass, don't beat, the immune system)
- **Field assignment:** Rename one scary component into ARB-boring language without
  changing a line of code.
- **Runtime:** ~24 min · **Status:** ◻ drafting

### Ep 07 — The Art of the Wedge
- **Source:** Ch 7 ("The Art of the Wedge" / *Winning the Beachhead*)
- **Boss:** The Procurement Committee (Budget Dust)
- **Physics:** `V_w` Wedge Velocity
- **Beat:** ZERO watches Sarah Chen's team burn 2.5 of 3.5 hours copy-pasting a
  SAR. Ships an ugly Excel plugin. Sarah leaves at 5:15 for the first time in two
  years. The wedge is planted; IT never sees it. Plant: Sarah's plugin.
- **Claim:** #3 + #5 (start where it's already bleeding)
- **Field assignment:** Find the one task a real person does 20× a week by hand.
  Wedge there. Bill it to budget dust.
- **Runtime:** ~22 min · **Status:** ◻ drafting

### Ep 08 — Good Trouble
- **Source:** Ch 8 ("Good Trouble" / *How to Be John Lewis Inside a Fortune 500*)
- **Boss:** Your Own Manager
- **Physics:** `GT` Good-Trouble quotient · `I_insurgent` Insurgent Index
- **Beat:** BISHOP's origin. Three years ago, Keisha Williams — 24, junior dev —
  fired for a working $2M tool built without ARB approval. That night BISHOP reads
  247 pages of policy, finds 23 loopholes, and spends three years using every one.
  His badge now hangs crooked. Plant: the crooked badge.
- **Claim:** #5 (good trouble is the only trouble worth making)
- **Field assignment:** Read the 247 pages nobody reads. Find your first loophole.
- **Runtime:** ~24 min · **Status:** ◻ drafting

### Ep 09 — Calculus for Cowards & The Qualification Protocol
- **Source:** Ch 9 ("Calculus for Cowards") + Ch 10 ("The Qualification Protocol")
- **Boss:** The CFO → the Innovation Lead
- **Physics:** `L_legacy` Legacy Cost · `S_fc` Safety Factor · Zombie Scorecard ·
  `I_v` Vaporware Index
- **Beat:** ZERO dumps the 22-slide deck at slide 3 and hands CFO Richard Park one
  sheet: cost of inaction, $12,290/day, $4.49M/yr. "Why hasn't anyone shown me this
  before?" Then FutureCorp inbound — buzzword soup, Zombie Score 0. She sends the
  Breakup Email; three days later the real VP calls, Score 59.
- **Claim:** #3 (reframe vision as liability refinancing) + qualification discipline
- **Field assignment:** Put a daily dollar figure on one status-quo. Score your
  hottest lead on the Zombie Scorecard; send the Breakup Email if it's a zombie.
- **Runtime:** ~28 min · **Status:** ◻ drafting

### Ep 10 — Weaponizing Bureaucracy
- **Source:** Ch 11 ("Weaponizing Bureaucracy" / *The Compliance Shield*)
- **Boss:** The CISO
- **Physics:** `D_audit` Audit Defensibility. Compliance Inheritance; Synthetic Data.
- **Beat:** REAPER flags ZERO as "anomalous vendor behavior"; an audit opens. She

- **Runtime:** ~24 min · **Status:** ◻ drafting

## MODULE III — THE INVASION *(Act 3: confrontation & choice)*

### Ep 11 — The Invasion Plan *(the climax)*
- **Source:** Ch 12 ("The Invasion Plan" / *From Shadow to System of Record*)
- **Boss:** The Legacy Vendor
- **Physics:** `T_r` Trust/Risk. The 4-Phase Protocol.
- **Beat:** Friday 5:07 PM, holiday weekend, skeleton crew. ZERO's shadow system
  detects a coordinated supply-chain attack the legacy misses — $4.2M exposure.
  15 seconds: stay safe in shadow, or reveal and save MegaCorp. She touches the
  implant scar and pushes the alert. Attack contained. BISHOP arrives — badge
  perfectly straight for the only time. CIO: "How fast can you start?" Risk inverts.
- **Claim:** the whole protocol paying off at once. Harvest: the crooked badge.
- **Field assignment:** Design your 15-second reveal now, before the Friday it
  matters. Know your `T_r` line.
- **Runtime:** ~30 min · **Status:** ◻ drafting

### Ep 12 — Field Dispatches: Three Runs in the Wild
- **Source:** Ch 13 (Fortress Run / banking) + Ch 14 (Paperclip Maximizer /
  healthcare) + Ch 15 (Propaganda Farm / ethics) — expanded to full dispatches
- **Boss:** three industries' immune systems
- **Physics:** applied review of the whole toolkit across three verticals.
- **Beats:**
  - **Fortress Run** — NeoFraud into Goliath Bank through Sarah's bleeding neck;
    19 months first-contact to System of Record; $6.89M first-year on a $1.8M
    contract. Harvest: Sarah's plugin math.
  - **Paperclip Maximizer** — MediMind enters St. Mary's via billing; the claims
    bot's clinical sidecar catches a coagulation disorder and saves James Morton's
    knee surgery. $36K wedge → $480K platform. Dr. Vance's reckoning.
  - **Propaganda Farm** — SIGNAL at TruthEngine builds an Ethical Sidecar via the
    Boring Revolution, documents the Dorian Gray Index in a GDPR-required DPIA. The
    system's own bureaucracy becomes the weapon. Good trouble without a megaphone.
- **Claim:** all five, demonstrated. The "it works in the wild" payoff.
- **Field assignment:** Pick the dispatch closest to your industry. Map its wedge
  onto your account.
- **Runtime:** ~32 min · **Status:** ◻ drafting

### Ep 13 — Victory Without Fire & The Insurgent's Oath
- **Source:** Ch 16 ("Victory Without Fire" / *The Morning After*)
- **Boss:** none — the morning after
- **Physics:** the four-percent math. The implant goes quiet.
- **Beat:** ZERO on the MegaCorp roof at night, GHOST beside her. She didn't burn

  billboard flickers: ONLY THE WEIRD SURVIVES. Harvest: the implant (Ch 1) goes
  quiet; the portrait (Prologue) heals.
- **Course frame:** **The Insurgent's Oath** — five commitments. Final SPZ closer.
- **Field assignment:** Take the Oath. Name your four percent and the date you'll
  measure it.
- **Runtime:** ~24 min · **Status:** ◻ drafting
