— by Space Pirate Zero. Business strategy × cyberpunk graphic novel × spec-ops
field manual. A transmission from Spaceship Alpha 9 ([UNIVERSE.md](../../UNIVERSE.md)).

novel score 8.0. Print, ebook, KDP, LinkedIn, and Substack all launched.

## Canon guardrails (RULES.md §9)

- REAPER is an **environment**, never a character/body/speech-bubble.
- No chapter defeats/destroys PRISM/REAPER (violates Claim 4).
- Punch up at the system, never down at workers.
- Influences woven heavily and honestly: Diogenes' "deface the currency" is
  the spine; Wilde's Dorian Gray central; Mamdani the authenticity case study.
