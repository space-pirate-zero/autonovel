# Digital Insurgency — 2nd Edition Revision Map

How the 1E manuscript changes to become the audio field course. This is the
content plan the recording scripts (`audiobook/scripts/ep_NN.md`) execute against.
The 1E chapters stay in `chapters/` as the source of truth; the 2E work lives as
episode scripts + the edits listed here.

## Principles

1. **Re-cut, don't rewrite the story.** The Broadcast plot, characters, and beats
   are locked. We adapt *for the ear* and *for episodic delivery* — we don't
   change what happens.
2. **Every equation gets spoken.** Symbol → spoken form → benchmark in words →
   ≥1 worked field report read as a dispatch. (See `audiobook/README.md`.)
3. **Every episode is self-contained.** A first-time listener dropping in on any
   episode gets a whole lesson; the sequence rewards bingers. That means light
   re-establishment at each cold open (who ZERO is, where we are) without recap
   bloat.
4. **Preserve the five foreshadow threads** (F1–F5 in `podcast/episodes.md`).
   Grouped episodes must not drop a plant or a payoff.
