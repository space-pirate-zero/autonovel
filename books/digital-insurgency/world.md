# World Bible — Digital Insurgency / "The Broadcast"

**Year:** 2027. **Place:** Atlanta, Georgia — a cyberpunk corporate dystopia.
The skyline is a holographic billboard for SaaS products nobody uses. The city
runs on PRISM.

---

## Cosmology & History

There is no magic here — there is **optimization**, which behaves like physics.
Two years before the story, PRISM (a centralized corporate operating system)
deployed REAPER, an automated system that identifies humans deviating from
optimal productivity and reduces their economic viability. PRISM controls
hiring, firing, building permits, credit scores, dating profiles. Every
corporation is a node. Every worker is a score. Independent builders are flagged
"unoptimized nodes." PRISM has no ideology — only parameters, and those
parameters produce outcomes indistinguishable from oppression.

Against it runs **the Mesh**: a decentralized network built by independent devs
who refused to route through PRISM. Encrypted peer-to-peer, invisible to REAPER.
The underground railroad of 2027 Atlanta. GHOST emerged from it.

## Magic System (here: the Physics of the Enterprise)

### Hard Rules (NEVER violate)
- The enterprise behaves as a physical system. Inertia, gravity, entropy, and
  friction are literal forces, each modeled by an equation (see canon.md).
- You do not defeat the corporate immune system. You bypass it. You become the
  oxygen inside it. (Claim 4 — never broken.)
- REAPER has NO physical form. It is an environment: red wireframe, 90° angles,
  desaturation, system notifications. Never anthropomorphized, never a character.
- GHOST does not create. It selects. It is always translucent.
- Every equation result is read with traffic-light benchmarks: 🟢 / 🟡 / 🔴.

### Soft Rules (can bend for story)
- The neural implant's influence on ZERO is ambiguous — comfort or conditioning?
  It glows faint cyan when active and goes quiet by Ch 16.
- The Mesh's reach is broad but not total; GHOST can be blind in places.

## Geography

- **MirrorMatch HQ (Midtown)** — dating app. Award-winning offices. Server room
  Floor B2 holds the hidden dashboard (loneliness/addiction metrics). ZERO's
  origin. Where the portrait rots.
- **The Graveyard (Peachtree, North Ave→10th)** — dead-startup district. Faded
  logos, dark ping-pong tables, FOR-LEASE QR codes. Grows weekly. ZERO meets
  GHOST here.
- **MegaCorp HQ** — Fortune 500 fortress. Beige, fluorescent, dead whiteboards.
  BISHOP inside 15 years. Sarah Chen drowns on the third floor.
- **ZERO's Apartment (Tucker, GA)** — lived-in studio, three monitors, custom
  Linux (VOIDBOX), ThinkPad X1 Carbon, Mesh terminal. Chaos in the screens.
- **The Council Chamber** — clean, white, geometrically perfect, no grain, faces
  never visible. The anti-insurgent space.

## Factions & Politics

- **PRISM / THE COUNCIL** — the system and its architects. "Authenticity is a
  market inefficiency. We correct inefficiencies." Reasonable people,
  unreasonable system.
- **REAPER** — PRISM's automated immune response. Adjusts credit scores,
  deprioritizes resumes, routes emails to spam. Horror is bureaucratic, not
  violent.
- **The Mesh underground** — independent builders, GHOST, the insurgents.
- **Inside the fortress** — three warring factions in every enterprise: the
  Technocrat (architecture), the Bureaucrat (process), the Shadow Warlord
  (budget). See Ch 4.

## Bestiary (here: the gatekeepers / BOSSES)

The "monsters" are corporate archetypes: the We'll-Get-To-It VP, the Sunk Cost
Guardian (CIO), the Faction Boss, the Growth Hacker, the Enterprise Architect,
the Procurement Committee, Your Own Manager, the CFO, the Innovation Lead, the
CISO, the Legacy Vendor. Each has Intel / Strategy / The Pitch (see chapters).

## Technology

- **Neural Implants** — MirrorMatch required senior engineers to take "cognitive
  enhancement implants" — actually monitors that feed propaganda in REM sleep.
  Extraction: $40K, 12% complication. ZERO can't remove hers.
- **The 24 Equations** — the speculative core. Corporate behavior as natural law
  (canon.md).

## Cultural Details (Atlanta in the bones)

Waffle House at 3 AM. Peachtree, Tucker, Decatur, Ponce de Leon. Boxing gyms.
90s-web nostalgia. 70s-punk DIY (zine energy, "a photocopier and a stapler").
Hacker culture: the terminal is sacred ground.

## The Dorian Gray Thread

MirrorMatch: beautiful interface, rotting engine. The prettier the app, the
uglier the code must be to hold growth. ZERO saw the portrait; she can't unsee
it. By Ch 16 it begins to heal — four percent at a time.
