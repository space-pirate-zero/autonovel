# Ep 05 — The Portrait of Dorian App

*The product stays beautiful. The portrait in the server room rots. And the beauty is a prerequisite for the ugliness.*

This is the moral core of the course — the Dorian Gray metaphor made literal. Two numbers carry it: the Dorian Gray Index (D_gray), how far your product's face has drifted from its engine, and Soul-Cost (S_soul), what building the wrong thing costs the person building it. Then the fix: the Ethical Sidecar. The story: a flashback to six months before the Prologue, the MirrorMatch war room, an A/B test where manufactured scarcity beats the honest notification by 23% — and ZERO watching the user-research videos where the suffering is real. The day she decided to quit.

**Field assignment:** Run the Mirror Test on your current sprint, compute a rough D_gray, and if it's climbing, build the Ethical Sidecar.
