# Ep 02 — The Rewrite

*Every line of code on earth has an expiration date. You just can't read yours yet.*

The rewrite is coming whether you're ready or not — and when anyone can generate competent, the only currency left is the human fingerprint. This episode gives you two ways to measure that: the Extinction Rate (R_ext), how fast generic work dies, and the Authenticity Half-Life (A_hl), how long your output stays yours before it's replicable. The story: the Graveyard of dead SaaS on Peachtree, where GHOST flickers in for the first time with the line that runs through the whole course — "They didn't die because they were bad. They died because they were generic. Only the weird survives."

**Field assignment:** Compute your A_hl — is your work replicable by Tuesday?
