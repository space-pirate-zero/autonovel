# Ep 07 — The Art of the Wedge

*Don't storm the fortress. Find the one wall that's already bleeding, and ship an ugly plugin into the crack.*

The wedge is how you get in before anyone can stop you — measured as Wedge Velocity (V_w): how fast a small, ugly tool proves value where the pain is loudest. Start where it's already bleeding, and bill it to budget dust so procurement never wakes up. The story: ZERO watches Sarah Chen's team burn 2.5 of 3.5 hours copy-pasting a report by hand. She ships an ugly Excel plugin. Sarah leaves at 5:15 for the first time in two years. The wedge is planted, and IT never sees it.

**Field assignment:** Find the one task a real person does 20 times a week by hand, wedge there, and bill it to budget dust.
