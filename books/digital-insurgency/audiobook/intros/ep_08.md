# Ep 08 — Good Trouble

*Read the 247 pages nobody reads. The rules that trap you are also 23 loopholes wide.*

Good trouble is the only kind worth making — quietly, on purpose, inside the rules. This episode gives you two gauges: the Good-Trouble quotient (GT) and the Insurgent Index (I_insurgent), the difference between reckless and effective disobedience. The story is BISHOP's origin. Three years ago, Keisha Williams — 24, junior dev — got fired for a working $2M tool she built without ARB approval. That night BISHOP read 247 pages of policy, found 23 loopholes, and has spent three years using every one. His badge hangs crooked ever since.
