# Ep 01 — Orientation: The Mirror Test

*Whatever you shipped this week — open it up and look at the engine, not the gradient. Almost nobody looks. That's why almost nobody wins.*

Every company you'll try to change has an immune system: forms, committees, sunk cost, and a fear reflex older than the org chart. This is the frame before the physics. 3:14 AM in the MirrorMatch server room, a burned-out platform engineer finds the second dashboard — the one that doesn't glow up to the lobby — and her own handle in the git blame. That's the Mirror Test: before you smuggle anything into a company, look at what you're smuggling. A cooler UI on an exploitation engine makes you a decorator, not an insurgent. The episode also lays out the Five Claims the whole course hangs on.
