# Outline — Digital Insurgency

## Structure
Prologue + 16 chapters + 3 appendices. Target 55,000–65,000 words
(~3,200–3,800 words/chapter). Three acts. Every chapter follows the Chapter
Format Template (seed.txt). Each chapter reinforces one or more of the Five Claims.

## Themes
The rewrite is coming; authenticity is the currency; the enterprise is physics;
the immune system is bypassed not beaten; good trouble is the only trouble worth
making. The Dorian Gray portrait runs throughout.
