Metcalfe's Law of friction.

A 10-person meeting has 45 lines of tension. Every head you add multiplies paths to 'no' and adds none to 'yes.' Committees are graveyards. Keep the room small enough to fit a decision.

The moral of the story: Every person you add to a decision multiplies the ways to say no and adds none to say yes. Shrink the room to the one who owns the pain; let the rest simply benefit.
